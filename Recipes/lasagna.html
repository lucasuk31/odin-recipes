What do I need to prepare it?

  • 500g Cheese
  • 500g Ham
  • Lasagna's pasta
  • 2 Cream cheese
  • Form to use inside the oven
  • Oven
  • Salt

Step by Step

  1. Pre heat the oven in 180 degrees
  2. Put the first layer Lasagna's pasta inside the form
  3. Put half of the cheese
  4. Put half of the ham
  5. Put 1 cream cheese
  6. Put another layer Lasagna's pasta
  7. Put the rest of the cheese
  8. Put the rest of the ham
  9. Put another cream cheese
  10. Take to the oven for 30 minutes
