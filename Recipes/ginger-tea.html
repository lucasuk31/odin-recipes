What do I need to prepare it?

  • 3 small pieces of Ginger
  • 1 lemon
  • half a liter of water
  • A pan
  • A cup

Step by Step

  1. Peel the ginger
  2. Put it inside the pan with half a liter of water
  3. Leave it with water inside the pan on a high heat for 15 minutes
  4. Take a lemon and squeeze it on a cup
  5. Switch off the fire and leave it rest for 5 minutes
  6. Put the water with the ginger inside the cup with lemon
